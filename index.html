Biography

Khaligraph Jones is a Kenyan rapper known for his powerful lyrics, deep voice, and influence in African hip-hop. He has won multiple awards and is recognized as one of the top rappers in Africa.

  • Real Name: Brian Ouko Omollo
  • Genre: Hip-Hop / Rap
  • Country: Kenya
  • Awards: BET Hip Hop Award Winner

Khaligraph Jones is known for his authenticity, storytelling, and dedication to uplifting African rap on the global stage.
