Biography

The Lyrical King

Khaligraph Jones (Brian Ouko Robert) is a Kenyan hip-hop heavyweight, widely regarded as one of the most skilled lyricists from East Africa. With a razor-sharp flow and profound storytelling, he has redefined the Kenyan rap scene.

From his early days in Kayole to winning multiple Best Hip-hop Artist awards, Khaligraph's journey is pure grit. His music blends authentic Swahili and English bars with hard-hitting beats, addressing social realities, ambition, and resilience.

Known for classic albums like "Testimony 1990" and "Invisible Currency", Khaligraph Jones continues pushing boundaries, collaborating with international stars and staying true to his roots.
